Cement Types

OPC & PPC Cement

We supply both Ordinary Portland Cement and Portland Pozzolana Cement across all major brands choose the right type for your application.

Grade 53
OPC Cement
Ordinary Portland Cement IS 269:2015

High-performance cement for demanding structural applications. Its rapid strength gain makes it ideal for pre-stressed concrete, heavy-duty floors, bridges, and multi-storey construction. Available in Grade 43 and Grade 53.

? Exceptional early and ultimate compressive strength
? Superior workability with low water-cement ratio
? Consistent fineness for uniform hydration
? Reduced shrinkage and improved durability

General Use
PPC Cement
Portland Pozzolana Cement IS 1489 (Part 1):2015

Eco-friendly cement blended with fly ash for excellent long-term durability. Ideal for mass concreting, plastering, waterproofing applications, and general residential construction.

? Lower heat of hydration prevents thermal cracking
? Higher resistance to sulphate and alkali attack
? Improved impermeability and long-term strength
? Eco-friendly: utilizes fly ash, reduces carbon footprint
